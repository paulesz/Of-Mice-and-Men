ABOUT US

We are Of Mice & Me.An American metalcore band from Orange County, California.
Our band members consists of lead vocalist and bassist Aaron Pauley, guitarists Phil Manansala and Alan Ashby, and drummer Valentino Arteaga.
Our group was founded by Austin Carlile and Jaxin Hall in mid-2009 after Carlile's departure from Attack Attack!.
Since 2009, We has released five studio albums. Carlile departed from the band in December 2016 citing that a long term health condition prompted his exit.
After Carlile's departure the We continued to pursue creating music with Pauley as the band's bassist and lead vocalist.
